Master Definition (Async-Await + useEffect) — Desi Style:

"Jab JavaScript me koi kaam aisa ho jo turant na ho (jaise API call), 
to usse async-await ke zariye handle karte hain — takay JavaScript ruk na jaaye aur kaam bhi ho jaye.
Lekin React me agar aise kaam ko bina useEffect ke likh diya, to har render pe dobara chalega. 
Isiliye useEffect ka use karte hain jo React ko bolta hai ki 'render hone de bhai, 
fir me side me kaam kar lunga — aur sirf ek baar (agar dependency [] hai)'."
